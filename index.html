About me

I have been a huge tech enthusiast from a young age. I love figuring out how technology works and how I can use it to simplify my life and the lives of others. I attended college at UNC Charlotte and earned a bachelor's degree Exercise Science but realized that my passion for technology was greater than my love for healthcare. I spent time learning coding at home before taking the leap to enroll in the UNCC full stack coding boot camp to further improve my skills.

During the boot camp, I gained a wealth of knowledge on the Front-end of development using JavaScript, ReactJS, and Jquery.

As for the Back-end, I discovered that I was very interested in learning the processes behind setting up a server and managing API routes that connect to databases such as MongoDB and MySQL. I also have experience using NodeJS, and Express.

I love to talk about tech and meeting new people. Feel free to contact me if you want to discuss anything further or just to talk about technology!
